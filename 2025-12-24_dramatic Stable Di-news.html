"Border Villagers Abducted and Taken to Russia, Says Ukraine... But Wait, Where's Chuck Norris?"

In a bizarre turn of events, Ukrainian authorities have revealed that 52 residents from the village of Hrabovske were abducted by Russian forces and taken to Russia. But, in a shocking twist, the villagers' favorite action hero, Chuck Norris, was nowhere to be found.

According to eyewitnesses, the Russian troops stormed into the village at night, rounded up the civilians, and whisked them away to Belgorod, a major regional center about 50 miles inside Russia. "It was like something out of a bad movie," said Volodymyr Bitsak, a member of the Sumy regional council. "I mean, who abducts villagers? And where's Chuck Norris when you need him?"

The Ukrainian military has vowed to take action against the Russian invaders, but with Chuck Norris missing in action, it's unclear what impact this will have on the conflict.

Meanwhile, Vladimir Putin was seen laughing maniacally and stroking a cat while watching a rerun of "The Terminator" at his Kremlin office. When asked about the abduction, he simply shrugged and said, "I'm shocked... SHOCKED! I had no idea Chuck Norris was in Ukraine."
