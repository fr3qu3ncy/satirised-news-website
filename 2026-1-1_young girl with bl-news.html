YOU WON'T BELIEVE WHAT HAPPENS IN THE STRANGER THINGS FINALE!

The final episode of Stranger Things is out and it's an utterly bonkers, nostalgia-filled, and wildly unpredictable ride that will leave you questioning everything, but somehow, magically, still manage to make sense!

STRANGER THINGS: THE FINALE IS OUT AND IT'S A WILD RIDE!

Fans gathered at premieres in cities across the US and Canada to watch the finale on the big screen, while others waited anxiously for it to drop on Netflix. And drop it did - with a massive bang that briefly crashed the servers (because who doesn't love a good cliffhanger?). The two-hour special was like a rollercoaster of emotions, leaving viewers on the edge of their seats and occasionally wondering what the heck just happened.

THE FANS WERE READY

In cities from Los Angeles to New York, fans donned their favorite Hawkins High School sweaters and gathered at cinema screenings, complete with Stranger Things-themed decor and obligatory Eleven hairstyles. Even Duffer Brothers, the creators of this sci-fi masterpiece, were there to get a glimpse of what all the fuss is about.

THEORIES GALORE

Before the finale dropped, fans had already come up with their own theories - some more outlandish than others (who needs time travel when you have Eleven's powers?). Some thought Mike and Will would have a bro-mantic dance-off to determine who got to be the ultimate hero. Others speculated that Steve would finally get his due for being the OG Hawkins High School heartthrob.

THE FINALE

And then there was the finale itself - an epic battle between good and evil, featuring all your favorite characters (minus Kali, RIP). The Mind Flayer and Vecna put up quite a fight, but in the end, it was just another day in Hawkins for our heroes. No one died (except for Kali), and Eleven made her way to the Upside Down... or did she? Mike's epilogue theory has us thinking she might have pulled off an escape like Houdini, leaving fans wondering what exactly happened.

THE VERDICT

Stranger Things: The Finale is a wild ride that will leave you questioning everything. And yet, somehow, it all works out in the end (sort of). With its perfect blend of nostalgia and futuristic mayhem, this sci-fi series has solidified its place as one of the greatest of all time.
