It's unclear what kind of superpower this gives her, but it's definitely not something you can learn in a self-defense class.

This was after he'd been having coffee nearby, because that's just how you start your day – with a cuppa and a side of terrorism prevention. Unfortunately for Ahmed, his heroics came at a cost: multiple gunshot wounds and possibly losing his arm.

They used these boards to... well, we're not entirely sure what they did with them – possibly as stretchers? But hey, it looked impressive!

That is, until lifeguard Davey spotted him and dragged him back to shore, where he promptly forgot all about his near-death experience and went on to tell the BBC how "chill" everything was.
