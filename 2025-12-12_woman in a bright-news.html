LONDON, UK - In a shocking turn of events, Iranian security forces have arrested Nobel Peace Prize winner and women's rights activist Narges Mohammadi, her supporters say. But here's the twist: they claim she was taken by aliens.

Yes, you read that right. According to sources close to the situation (okay, it's just her husband), Ms. Mohammadi was attending a memorial service for a deceased lawyer when suddenly, out of nowhere, a spaceship descended from the sky and beamed her up. "I'm pretty sure she was abducted by aliens," said Taghi Rahmani, her husband. "I mean, have you seen her face? She's got that 'I've been probed' look going on."

The Iranian government has yet to comment on the allegations, but sources within the country's secret space program (yes, that's a thing) claim that Ms. Mohammadi is being held at an extraterrestrial holding facility deep in the heart of Tehran.

Iranian Activist Says Country Threatening Her with 'Physical Elimination' - and Pizza Delivery

But what really got her in trouble was when she ordered a large pepperoni pizza from Domino's and they added jalapenos without asking.

"I'm not sure what I did to deserve such treatment," said Ms. Mohammadi, still recovering from her intergalactic ordeal. "I mean, who doesn't love a good jalapeno on their pizza? It's like they're trying to silence me with spice... and aliens."
