MODI'S CONGRATULATORY MESSAGE: A CAUTIONARY TALE

In a message posted on social media, Modi sent his warmest congratulations to BNP leader Tarique Rahman, complete with a photo of himself and Rahman's mom, Khaleda Zia. The tone was eerily reminiscent of a 70s disco dance hit - all smooth moves and no substance.

"Hey, hey, BNP, it's time to party! We're happy to see you in power, Tarique bhai," Modi tweeted, using the Indian term for "brother." "We'll work together to strengthen our multifaceted relationship... but only if you promise not to attack any more Hindus or stash arms for rebels."

SHEIKH HASINA'S EXILE: THE PLOT THICKENS

Living in exile in Delhi, Sheikh Hasina is now facing a death sentence in absentia for her alleged role in the 2024 security crackdown. India's refusal to extradite her has further complicated things with Bangladesh. One wonders if she'll be allowed to stay at the Indian embassy as long as she behaves and doesn't make too many demands, like "Can I have some of that delicious Delhi street food?" or "Hey, can you guys please tell me what happened to my favorite scarf?"
