"I mean, seriously, who needs a 'superhero' cop when you can have a pair of evil masterminds trying to take over the world?" Akram grumbled, before being hauled away by authorities for plotting more dastardly schemes.

In conclusion, Jack Hibbert's homecoming is a true Christmas miracle – a testament to the power of hope, redemption, and really bad life choices.
