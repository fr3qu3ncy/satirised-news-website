BREAKING: Zohran Mamdani Abandons Rent-Stabilised Apartment for Gracie Mansion, but Not Before it Becomes a Hotspot for Uninvited Guests

"I'm not saying it's true," said Mamdani in a statement on Monday, "but I've heard rumors that the mansion is haunted by the spirits of past mayors. Maybe it's just my imagination, but I keep seeing a ghostly figure of Michael Bloomberg wandering around, muttering something about 'affordable housing' and 'tax cuts'."

It seems that Mamdani will be trading in his modest digs for a life of luxury and mystery at Gracie Mansion.
