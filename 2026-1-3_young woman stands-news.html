The condo market is experiencing a historic crash, with smaller units - those under 600 sq ft - taking the biggest hit. THE CAUSES: A RECIPE FOR DISASTER

So, what went wrong? Well, it turns out that developers got a bit too excited and built way more micro-condos than people actually wanted to buy. It's like they thought, "Hey, let's build a whole bunch of tiny condos for hipsters who just want to Instagram their avocado toast!" Little did they know, the hipsters would soon grow up and realize that their 300 sq ft condo was basically a glorified dorm room.
