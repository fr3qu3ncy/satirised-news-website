You Won't Believe What This Guy Did With A $19,000 Fabergé Egg...

A man tried to steal a $19,000 Fabergé egg by eating it, and when that didn't quite work out as planned, he ended up "pooping it out" in a bizarre incident that left authorities and witnesses utterly bewildered.

BREAKING: Man Eats Fabergé Egg, Cops Recover in 'Natural Manner'

In a bizarre incident that has left authorities baffled, a man was arrested for allegedly trying to steal a $19,000 diamond-studded egg named after the James Bond film Octopussy. But what's even more astonishing is that he tried to swallow it whole.

According to sources, the 32-year-old man walked into Partridge Jewellers in central Auckland, New Zealand, and stuffed the Fabergé egg locket into his mouth. Witnesses claim he then tried to make a run for it, but was quickly apprehended by police.

"It was like something out of a bad action movie," said one witness. "I mean, who tries to steal a Fabergé egg by eating it? It's just not how you do things."

Police say the man has been charged with theft and is currently being held in custody. But in a surprising twist, they announced that the Fabergé egg had been recovered in a "natural manner" – meaning the guy literally pooped it out.

"We're happy to report that the suspect has undergone a medical assessment and is no longer a biohazard," said a spokesperson for the police department. "We're just relieved that nobody was hurt... except maybe the guy's stomach lining."

The Fabergé egg, which features an 18-carat gold miniature octopus and is set with 60 white diamonds and 15 blue sapphires, will reportedly be returned to its rightful owner – a certain Bond villain, no doubt.
