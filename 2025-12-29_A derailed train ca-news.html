TRAIN-WRECK OF A STORY

According to eyewitnesses, the train was traveling at breakneck speeds, with passengers playing bingo and drinking margaritas in the aisles. It was like a cross between a Mexican fiesta and a high-speed chase scene from The Fast and the Furious.

As the train rounded a bend near the town of Nizanda (which we're pretty sure is not actually a real place), it suddenly veered off course, jumped over a cliff, and landed in a nearby taco truck. Yes, you read that right - a taco truck!

The driver of the taco truck, who wished to remain anonymous (because, let's be real, they were probably trying to get out of serving tacos on the day), said: "I was just trying to deliver some delicious carnitas to the good people of Nizanda when suddenly this train comes flying over and lands in my kitchen! It was like a scene from a bad sci-fi movie!"
