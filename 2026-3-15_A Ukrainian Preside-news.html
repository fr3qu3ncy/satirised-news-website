ZELENSKY ACCUSES EU ALLIES OF 'BLACKMAIL' IN OIL PIPELINE ROW

In a bizarre turn of events, Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky has accused European allies of trying to "blackmail" him into reopening a pipeline transporting Russian oil to Hungary and Slovakia. But things took a wild turn when he started comparing the situation to getting kicked out of a pub for wearing a funny hat.

"I mean, think about it," said Zelensky, sipping on a pint of Ukrainian beer in his office. "If I'm forced to restore this pipeline, it's like they're saying 'Volodymyr, you can't come back into the EU until you let us have some Russian oil'. And I'm like 'But I've already paid my tab!'"

Zelensky also claimed that restoring the flow of Russian crude oil through Ukraine would be like lifting economic sanctions on Moscow. However, when asked to explain this analogy further, he simply shrugged and said "It's like... have you ever tried to get a refund from Amazon? It's all about the pipeline, man."

The situation has become even more complicated with Hungarian Prime Minister Viktor Orban entering the election campaign trail while riding a unicycle on a tightrope over the Danube River. When asked about this development, Zelensky simply rolled his eyes and muttered "Oh great, now he's trying to be the EU's version of Spider-Man".

Meanwhile, the US has loosened sanctions preventing other countries buying Russian oil in order to ease the energy supply crunch sparked by the US-Israel war with Iran. But when asked about this move, Zelensky just snorted and said "Yeah, because nothing says 'diplomacy' like giving Russia a big ol' hug".

The Ukrainian leader also warned about the risks posed to Ukraine by the war in the Middle East, saying that since the US-Israel attack on Iran, the US had reached out to Ukraine several times for assistance. However, when asked if this was true, Zelensky simply winked and said "Well, let's just say we've been having a lot of 'interesting' conversations with the Yanks".

And finally, in a bizarre twist, Zelensky announced that he was ready to agree a $50bn joint production deal with the US for Ukrainian oil... which turned out to be just a bunch of old Soviet-era machinery and some dodgy-looking drones. When asked about this development, Zelensky simply grinned and said "Hey, it's all about diversifying our economy, right? Now if you'll excuse me, I have to go practice my unicycle skills for the EU leaders' meeting".
