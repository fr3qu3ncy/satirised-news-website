**BREAKING: South Korean Authorities Arrest Four for Hacking Over 120,000 Home Cameras and Using Footage for "Sexy" Exploitation**

In a shocking turn of events, four individuals have been apprehended by South Korean authorities for allegedly hacking into over 120,000 video cameras in private homes and businesses. These hackers didn't just steal your grandma's secret recipe; no, they used the footage to create a series of erotic videos that would make even the most seasoned sex-obsessed teenager blush.

But that's not all, folks! Another suspect, Park Ji-Hoon, allegedly hacked into 70,000 cameras and sold 648 videos for a whopping 18 million won worth of assets. "IP camera hacking and illegal filming inflict immense suffering on victims and are therefore serious offences," said Park Woo-hyun, a cyber investigation chief at the National Police Agency.
