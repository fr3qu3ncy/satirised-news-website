DEADLY TEXAS BAR SHOOTING: A COMEDY VERSION

In a shocking turn of events, two people were killed and 14 injured in a shooting outside a beer garden in Austin, Texas. But don't worry, folks, it's not all doom and gloom. I mean, who needs a functioning society when you can have a good bar fight?

The suspected gunman, Ndiaga Diagne (aka "N- Danger" to his friends), was identified as a naturalized American citizen born in Senegal. Or, as we like to call him, the "World's Okayest Assassin." Diagne's motives remain unclear, but sources close to the investigation say he was probably just trying to get a good seat at the bar.

As the sun rose over Austin (aka "The Live Music Capital of the World... until the shooting started"), police responded to calls of an active shooter around 2:00 am. But instead of rushing in with guns blazing, they decided to have a nice game of dodgeball with the suspect. It was a close match, but in the end, the cops emerged victorious.

The SUV Diagne used as his getaway car (aka "The Most Obvious Suspect Vehicle Ever") was searched and found to be carrying no explosives... just a few cases of Lone Star beer. Because, priorities.

Speaking of priorities, US President Donald Trump was notified of the shooting. And by "notified," I mean he was woken up from his afternoon nap by his aides, who said, "Hey, Donnie, someone got shot in Texas." He promptly tweeted, "Sad! (And also, can someone get me a beer?)"

Meanwhile, Republican Senator Ted Cruz condemned the attack, saying, "What happened early this morning in Austin was a senseless act of violence... and also a great opportunity to talk about my new book, 'The Art of Not Getting Assassinated'."
