India has ordered all new smartphones to come pre-loaded with a state-run cybersecurity app, because who needs privacy when you can have the government watching your every move? In other words, you can't even uninstall it without using a crowbar.

The move has been criticized by cyber experts, who say it breaches citizens' right to privacy. But let's be real, who needs that when you can have the government knowing everything about your browsing history?
