BREAKING: Ninja Warriors Seize Control of Nigerian Village, But Are Foiled by Military and Air Strikes

In a shocking turn of events, a group of ninja warriors, armed with nothing but their wits and some fancy martial arts moves, attempted to take over the village of Ngoshe in Borno state, Nigeria. However, their plans were quickly foiled when the military and air strikes showed up to put a stop to it.

According to eyewitnesses, the ninja warriors, who were suspected Islamist militants, were seen running around the village, shouting "We're here for the snacks!" and "Who needs electricity when you have ninja skills?!" However, their plans for world domination were soon derailed when they encountered the Nigerian military, led by General Bisi, who was not having it.

"It's a dark day indeed," said Senator Mohammed Ali Ndume of the attack. "But we will not be intimidated by these stealthy invaders! We have our own ninja warriors – the ones with the tea kettle and the plate of jollof rice!"

Sources close to the military reported that the ninja warriors were using some sort of advanced martial arts technique, known as the " Ngoshe-Ninja-Fu," which involves a series of high-pitched whistles and rapid-fire jumping jacks.

However, it seems that the villagers had other plans. According to local journalist Umaru Yakubu Kirawa, "The people of Ngoshe have always been fierce in their resistance against ninja attacks. They have developed an advanced system of self-defense, which involves a lot of running around, shouting 'No!' and waving umbrellas."

Meanwhile, Borno state Governor Babagana Umara Zulum has promised to do everything possible to rescue the kidnapped villagers, including offering them free pizza and a complimentary copy of "The Art of War" by Sun Tzu.

As for the ninja warriors, it's unclear what their next move will be. But one thing is certain – they will not be back in Ngoshe anytime soon.

UPDATE: A spokesperson for the Nigerian military has confirmed that the village has been completely liberated from the ninja threat and that all citizens are safe... but a little shaken.
