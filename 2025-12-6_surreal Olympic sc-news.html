When asked what he was doing, Borat allegedly replied, "I am here to show you my incredible skills at making funny faces for camera. You will be laughing so hard, your belly will ache like Russian belly dancer after drinking too much vodka."

As the Winter Olympics 2026 are just around the corner, officials are scrambling to prepare for the influx of international visitors. "It's a tough job," said one official on condition of anonymity. "Those cats are like furry little ninjas. They can sneak up and steal the flame without anyone even noticing."

The Winter Olympics 2026 are set to take place in February, but with Borat and the squirrels involved, it's clear that things are about to get interesting.
