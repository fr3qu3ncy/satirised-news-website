BREAKING: Hamas Commander Killed in Gaza Strike, Israel Blamed (Sort Of)

According to eyewitnesses (i.e., the guy who was driving behind him), Saad's car was parked near a small café where Barbra Streisand was performing an impromptu concert. Witnesses claim that just as Babs finished her rendition of "You Don't Bring Me Flowers," a blast rocked the area, killing Saad and three others. We're pretty sure it had nothing to do with the fact that he was wearing a "I Hamas" t-shirt.

Meanwhile, Hamas officials are pointing fingers and accusing Israel of "yet another heinous act of terrorism." We're pretty sure they mean it this time. In fact, we've just received word that a group of Hamas militants have taken over the Gaza Strip's only IKEA store in protest. Yes, you read that right – IKEA.
