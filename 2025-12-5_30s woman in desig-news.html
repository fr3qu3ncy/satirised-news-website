The courtroom erupted into chaos as Yamagami's lawyers began arguing over who got to claim the extra-large coffee mug that Abe's widow had brought with her, only to have it snatched by none other than Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson, who was sitting in the front row wearing a "I'm not here for the drama" t-shirt.
