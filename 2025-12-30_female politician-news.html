As the first female leader of Bangladesh, Khaleda Zia was determined to make a name for herself and bring some much-needed stability to the country. She was like a ninja, but with more sarees and less stealth. Her plan? To introduce free primary education, mandatory for all children over 5 years old... unless they were allergic to pencils.
