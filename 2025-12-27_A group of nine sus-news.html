It's like they raided the set of "Money Heist" or something!

One of the most well-known suspects is Mohammad Hannoun, the president of the Palestinian Association in Italy, who has previously denied allegations that he was involved with Hamas (or at least, that's what he said before being carted off to prison).

Interior Minister Matteo Piantedosi thanked police for their work and reminded everyone that "the presumption of innocence must always be recognised at this stage." Yeah, because nothing says "innocence" like being accused of funding a terrorist organization.
