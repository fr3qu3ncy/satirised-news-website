**BBC Verify Tracks US Assets in Caribbean Sea, but Finds Them Playing Beach Volleyball Instead**

The US assets, which included a fleet of high-tech warships and a squadron of stealth bombers, were reportedly spotted doing backflips off the beach and attempting to dunk each other in a game of "dunk- volleyball." "We're not involved in anything illicit," said a spokesperson for the Venezuelan government. "We're just really into beach volleyball. Like, seriously into it."
